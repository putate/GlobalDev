. The following data contains records collected on different countries and geographic locations from 1980 - 2013 from the World Bank. Included is different data about urban development, agriculture and rural development, health, and infrastructure.


Birth rate is one of the statisitics measured for each country every year. BIRTH RATE is the number of resident live births for a specified geographic area (nation, state, county, etc.) during a specified period (usually a calendar year) divided by the total population (usually mid-year) for that area and multiplied by 1,000.

All countries are listed multiple times throughout the json file, this is because each listing of a country contains an entirely different set of data pertaining to the year that it is listed in.
